### UC 11: France-Canada Student Digital Identity (SIROS)

 Standards and protocols in scope, to be finalised in detailed design and aligned with the WP2 consolidated profile:

 - eIDAS 2.0 Regulation and related Implementing Acts.
 - EUDIW Architecture Reference Framework (ARF) — baseline version **[TBC, aligned with WP2 D2.1]**.
 - OpenID for Verifiable Credential Issuance (OpenID4VCI).
 - OpenID for Verifiable Presentations (OpenID4VP).
 - Credential formats: ISO/IEC 18013-5 mdoc and/or IETF SD-JWT VC — selection **[TBC]**.
 - W3C Verifiable Credentials Data Model, where applicable.
 - DIACC Pan-Canadian Trust Framework (PCTF) for Canadian-side assurance mapping.
 - Accessibility standards: EN 301 549 on the EU side; Quebec/Canadian equivalents on the Canadian side.
