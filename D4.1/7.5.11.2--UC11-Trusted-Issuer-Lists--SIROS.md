#### Trusted Issuer Lists

On the EU side, the French PID issuer and GYSC are published via the relevant EU trust lists and ARF-defined issuer registries. On the Canadian side, no equivalent central trusted-issuer list consumable by a European wallet exists today. UC 11 will prototype how the Canadian verifier resolves the trust status of an EU issuer — by consuming the EU trust list directly, via a mirror, or via a purpose-built bridge — and will document the outcome.
